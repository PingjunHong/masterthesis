- Ducks do not sleep while expanding their wings.
- Ducks typically do not sleep soundly when they are actively moving in water.
- Ducks need to be still or in a safe place to sleep soundly, not expanding wings.
- Ducks expanding their wings are usually awake and active.
- Sleeping ducks generally do not float with expanded wings.
- Expanding wings is an indication of wakefulness, not sleep.
- Ducks in motion, such as expanding wings, are not in a state of restful sleep.
